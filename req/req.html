Our Process For Adoption

Adoption Requirements (Eligibility Criteria)

Minimum age requirement (e.g., 21+ years)
Stable living conditions (renters may need landlord approval)
Financial capability to care for a pet
No history of animal abuse
Agreement to regular vet checkups and proper care

Adoption Process

Step 1:

Browse Pets – Explore our platform to find the perfect pet for you.

Step 2:

Submit Application – Complete a simple form to express your interest.

Step 3:

Screening Call – Join a quick call to discuss your adoption readiness.

Step 4:

Home Visit – (Optional) We may check your home virtually or in person

Step 5:

Meet & Greet – Spend time with the pet to see if it’s a good match.

Step 6:

Final Approval & Fee – Get approved and pay the adoption fee.

Step 7:

Bring Your Pet Home – Welcome your new pet into your family!

Documents Needed

Government ID

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I adopt if I live in a flat?

Yes! Many pets are well-suited for apartment living. Just ensure they get enough activity and attention.

Can students adopt?

It depends on the student's living conditions and long-term commitment. A screening call helps us decide suitability.

Is there a trial adoption period?

Yes, we offer a short trial period for some pets to ensure the match is right before finalizing.

Can I return the pet later?

Yes, we always prioritize the pet’s well-being. If things don’t work out, we help rehome them responsibly.
